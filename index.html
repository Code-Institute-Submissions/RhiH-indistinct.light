Indistinct Light:

a photographic exploration of how the world changes in darkness.

Image trails illuminate or temporarily blind the viewer, distorting what they can see around them, leading them to reconsider exactly what they think they just saw, blurry and indistinct. Peaceful, but wary as they move along.
All photographs were taken during late 2020-early 2021, reflecting the uneasy nature of the time.
